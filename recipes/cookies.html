Ingredients

  • butter
  • flour
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • brown sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • baking soda
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. melt the butter and mix with eggs, brown sugar, sugar and vanilla extract
  3. in a separate bowl, combine flour and baking soda
  4. slowly mix flour with wet mixture until fully combined
  5. add chocolate chips to mixture
  6. even separate dough and form into balls, placeo on cookie sheet
  7. once oven is ready, bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown
  8. let cool and enjoy!
