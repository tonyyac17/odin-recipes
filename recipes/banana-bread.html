Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • vanilla extract
  • bananas
  • butter
  • milk
  • chocolate chips
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. melt the butter and mix with eggs, milk, smashed bananas
  3. add baking soda, salt, vanilla extract and cinammon to liquid
  4. add flour and mix until combined, careful not to over mix
  5. add chocolate chips
  6. bake for 20 min
  7. let cool for 5 min and enjoy
